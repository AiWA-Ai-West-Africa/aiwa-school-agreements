# Reference — International Standards

This folder holds reference materials from international organisations whose frameworks
inform AIWA's governance, safeguarding, and educational standards.

> **Note:** References listed here represent frameworks that AIWA seeks to align with
> conceptually and in principle. Their inclusion does not constitute a claim of formal
> accreditation, certification, or verified legal compliance with these frameworks.

## Key International Frameworks

| Organisation | Framework | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **UNESCO** | Convention on the Safeguarding of Intangible Cultural Heritage (2003) | Cultural heritage protection principles |
| **UNESCO** | Recommendation on Open Educational Resources (2019) | Open educational materials |
| **UN** | Convention on the Rights of the Child (CRC) | Child safeguarding and participation |
| **UNICEF** | Child-Friendly Schools Framework | School programme standards |
| **UN** | Sustainable Development Goal 4 — Quality Education | Programme alignment |
| **African Union** | Continental Education Strategy for Africa (CESA) | Regional educational context |
| **WIPO** | Traditional Knowledge and Cultural Expressions guidance | Cultural heritage and community rights |
