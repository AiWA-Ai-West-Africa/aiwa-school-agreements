# Reference — MoBSE (Ministry of Basic and Secondary Education)

This folder holds reference materials related to the **Gambia Ministry of Basic and
Secondary Education (MoBSE)** policy frameworks, curriculum standards, and official
guidance that inform AIWA's school pilot programmes.

## Contents

Add reference documents here as they are gathered. Suggested items include:

- MoBSE school registration and partnership guidance
- National curriculum framework summaries
- School calendar and academic year references
- MoBSE contact and official communications directory
- Policy circulars relevant to external educational partners

## Note on Source Materials

Documents in this folder are **reference materials only**. They represent external
policy context and do not constitute AIWA policies or agreements. Always verify
currency with the MoBSE directly.
