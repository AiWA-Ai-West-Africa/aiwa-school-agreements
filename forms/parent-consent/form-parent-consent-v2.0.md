## AIWA PROGRAMME PERMISSION FORM
### For Parents and Guardians

---

Dear Parent / Guardian,

AI West Africa (AIWA) is working with **[SCHOOL_NAME]** to run **[PROGRAM_NAME]** for
students. All sessions take place at school. There are no fees.

---

### What Will Students Do?

- Read stories, poems, and other writing together
- Write their own stories, poems, and creative pieces
- Learn how books and publications are made
- Work together, share ideas, and build confidence in speaking and storytelling

---

### Important Points

- Joining is your child's choice. If you change your mind later, your child can leave the
  programme without any problem at school.
- Your child must only submit work they created themselves or have permission to use.
- Your child keeps ownership of their work. AIWA will ask for separate permission before any
  public sharing or publication.
- If you separately agree to extended use, AIWA and its affiliates, including Starisian
  Technologies, may use your child's work for language preservation, educational materials,
  translation, research, and approved language or AI tools. This is by licence only and does
  not transfer ownership.
- AIWA keeps records of authorship and permissions. Any payment or royalties for licensed use
  are handled under separate rights and licensing agreements.
- Students only use AIWA-approved tools built by Starisian Technologies for AIWA. Personal
  commercial third-party accounts are not required.
- Sessions are held at school in open, visible spaces and AIWA facilitators are vetted before
  working with students.
- Questions or safeguarding concerns: [AIWA_CONTACT_PHONE] | [AIWA_CONTACT_EMAIL]
